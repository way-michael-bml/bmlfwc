The Fire Hydrant Project

Advocating for the fire suppression infrastructure our community deserves

The Situation

When Nevada Irrigation District (NID) upgraded the water lines along Banner Lava Cap Road, the new pipe was pressurized with enough capacity to support fire hydrants — but hydrants weren't part of that project. The infrastructure we'd need is already in the ground; installing hydrants would be the natural next step.

Our Goal

We're circulating a petition asking NID to partner with our neighborhood on installing hydrants along this corridor, so our parcels have the fire suppression access the existing pipeline was built to support.
