The NID Hydrant Project

Demanding the fire suppression infrastructure our community deserves

The Issue

When water service was added along our main road, the Nevada Irrigation District (NID) overlooked installing fire hydrants. This gap in fire suppression capability puts our properties and lives at unnecessary risk.

The Project Goal

We are launching a petition to NID requesting them to correct this oversight and install the necessary hydrants to provide adequate fire suppression capabilities for our parcels.
